Wild Water Rafting Co.

At Wild Water Rafting Co., our creed is simple: the river teaches what the classroom cannot. We exist to turn nervous first-timers into confident paddlers, and to send every guest home a little braver, a little wetter, and a lot happier than when they arrived.

History

Wild Water Rafting Co. launched from the back of a battered pickup truck and a single rented raft, founded by a pair of river guides who couldn't imagine a desk job. What began as weekend trips for friends grew, one word-of-mouth referral at a time, into a full outfitting company with a fleet of rafts, a crew of certified guides, and a base camp perched right where the canyon narrows and the rapids pick up. Decades later, the mission hasn't changed: get people onto the water and let the river do the rest.
